Undergrad Roadmaps

4-Year Plan (The Power Plan)

  • Year 1: Take Gen Chem 1 & 2, Bio 1 & 2, and Physics 1 & 2. Join Pre-Dental club. Visit professors weekly in office hours to build Letters of Rec early. Ace classes so you can Tutor them next semester.
  • Year 2: OChem 1 & 2 and Physiology. Keep generals light. Continue volunteering and lead a group. Start Shadowing and find a part-time job in a dental office. In December: Schedule the DAT for May.
  • Year 3: Jan–April: Study for the DAT (3 months). April: Ask for Letters of Rec. May: Take the DAT. June 1st: Submit AADSAS Application. Finish with Biochem and Anatomy.
  • Year 4: Finish credits by April. Take Pathophys and Microbio. Attend Interviews. Start Dental School in Summer/Fall!

3-Year Plan (The Accelerated)

  • Year 1: Double up: General Chemistry 1 & 2, Biology 1 & 2, & Physics 1 & 2. Build relationships for Letters of Rec immediately.
  • Year 2: Organic Chemistry 1 & 2 & Biochemistry 1 & 2. Jan–April: Study for DAT. Take DAT in May. Shadow and work in a clinic.
  • Year 3: Apply June 1st. Complete Physiology and Manual Dexterity projects. Interview in Fall, graduate in Spring.

2-Year Plan (The Pivot)

  • Year 1: Sprint through General Chemistry 1 & 2, Biology 1 & 2, and Organic Chemistry 1 & 2 (Summer). High-intensity Shadowing (100+ hrs).
  • Year 2: Physics 1 & 2 & Physiology. Take DAT in May and Apply June 1st. Plan for a Gap Year to work as an assistant while interviewing.
